MADAN B. LOKUR, J.
This appeal is directed against the order dated 12th May,
2005 passed by the Income Tax Appellate Tribunal, Delhi Bench “F”,
New Delhi in ITA No. 1994 (Del) of 1999.

2.

The appeal arises out of penalty proceedings. In the quantum

appeal (ITA No.1315/2006), we have come to the conclusion that no
substantial question of law arises for consideration. Since the present
appeal is consequential, we are of the opinion that no substantial
question of law arises.

3.

The appeal is dismissed.
