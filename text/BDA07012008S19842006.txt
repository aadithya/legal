The plaintiff is the proprietor of the registered trademark

TIGER as well as device of TIGER and the artistic works vesting in the

product - Beer. The defendant was using the impugned marks / labels
BENGAL TIGER / BENGAL TIGER PREMIUM LARGER BEER /
BENGAL PREMIUM LAGER BEER alongwith the device of TIGER.

This application is signed by Mr V. Seetharaman who is the

the respective counsel of the parties. The application be marked as
Exhibit C-1.

5.

I have examined the terms of the settlement arrived at

between the parties and I am satisfied that the same are lawful.
Consequently, the settlement is taken on record. The suit is decreed in
favour of the plaintiff and against the defendant in terms of prayers (a),
(b), (c), (d) and (e) of paragraph 38 of the plaint. The suit is dismissed
in respect of the prayers contained in paragraph 38 (f), (g) and (h) of the
plaint. This application (Exhibit C-1) shall form part of the decree. The
